A Circle That Grows by Trust

You cannot ask to join. You are invited.

One guest may bring another only once and only if they are certain that person belongs.

An invitation is not a guarantee of return. Seats are taken, never held.

Those who come once are guests. Those who return become part of the fellowship.

A quiet circle that grows by trust, by recognition, by alignment.
